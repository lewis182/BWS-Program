Chest Program
10 weeks · 3-day split
Workout
Based on the BWS 10-Week Chest Program (3-day split) by Jeremy Ethier.
Cable, machine, band and pull-up exercises swapped for free-weight/bench alternatives (Pull-ups → Barbell Bent-Over Row).
Tutorial links open the illustrated guides hosted alongside this page. Progress saved locally under bws-chest-program-free-weights-v1.
